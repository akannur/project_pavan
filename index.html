About Us

Avyakta Food Exports LLP is a B2B food supplier/Exporter. We export food products like Cereals including all types of Rice, processed Cereals, Pulses, Flours, Spices Whole and powders, Sugar & Confectionary, Salts, Ayurvedic products, Nuts & Dried fruits, Gheee & Cooking oils, Masalas & Mixes, Tea, Coffee, Beverages & Beverage Mixes, Pickles & Papads, Coconuts products , Health products, Fried snacks, Cosmetics, Soaps, all types Pooja items, …..Phew….!!! We have a looooooong list of Products for you! Just name it…We have that for You.

One of the best part is Our Location. We operate in a city called Mysuru. Mysore is the Heritage City and the City of Palaces. It also is the Cleanest City in INDIA. Our city is in the Epicenter of South India - which is one of the biggest and finest contributors of Spices, Cereals, Coffee and other Food products. We are located near to the “Land of spices- Kerala”. No country in the world produces as many kinds of spices as India does.

We have a Strooooong Team with The Beeeeest Skills working for You. Whether it may be in The Quality department or May it be in The Production or The client Interaction, or any other for that matter. Our team prefers the Fineeest possible act.

We have our own In-House Lab!!! Yes, You read it right!!! We can certify You that every item delivered to you is of the Fineeeest Quality with the looooongest shelf life. We even can get your products tested by 3 rd party labs or specifically by NABL Labs. We abide to the Import Quality policies of every country, as each county has its own stringent Import Quality policies. We have Experts to take care of that for You. Quality is our Breath.
